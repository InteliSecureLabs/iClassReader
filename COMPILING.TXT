COMPILING
==========

Use Embarcadero® C++Builder® XE5 to load the CopyClass.cbproj file.

(Embarcadero® C++Builder® XE5 is available with a free 30-day trial).

Within Embarcadero look at the Menu bar across the top, choose Project,

then "Build CopyClass" from the drop-down menu.

A comiled executable 

You will need to copy lib/scardsyn.dll to the location of the compiled executable for it to correctly execute.
